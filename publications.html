Research

My research interests can be summed up as constrained optimization, where those constraints lie either in preserving data privacy or exploiting certain structures. My goals are to develop provably efficient algorithms under these contraints. Below are some of the projects I have been fortunate to be a part of during my time at Rutgers University.
