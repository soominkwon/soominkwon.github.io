About Me

I am a PhD student in the EECS Department at the University of Michigan-Ann Arbor, co-advised by Prof. Laura Balzano and Prof. Qing Qu. I completed my M.S. degree in the ECE Department at Rutgers University under Prof. Anand D. Sarwate, researching in learning from low-rank approximations of tensor data. I also obtained my B.S. degree in the ECE Department at Rutgers University with high honors and a minor in Mathematics. Lately, I am interested in generative models (e.g. diffusion models) as well as overparameterized models (e.g. deep neural networks) and the inherent bias in their learning dynamics towards low-rank solutions.
